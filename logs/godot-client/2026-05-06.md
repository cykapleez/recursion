# Godot Client Log — 2026-05-06

### — Enemy AI: Undead Colossus (AOE stomp + eye laser)

**Asked:** Add AI to the Undead Colossus with two attacks: (1) AOE stomp with a red circle indicator on the floor, 1-second windup before damage; (2) Eye laser with a red beam path indicator before firing.

**Done:**
- Added AI constants, state enum, and variables to `enemy.gd`.
- Implemented `_process(delta)` state machine: IDLE → WINDUP_AOE or WINDUP_LASER → back to IDLE.
- AOE attack: pulsing red transparent CylinderMesh disc (radius 4.5) placed at the player's ground position; after 1.4s windup, deals 35 damage to all players within radius; flashes the colossus body orange on land.
- Laser attack: pulsing red BoxMesh beam from eye midpoint to player, added to scene root (world space); after 1.4s windup, checks all players within 2.0 units of beam path; deals 55 damage to hit players; flashes body white on fire.
- Attack range: 22.0 units; cooldown: 4.0s after each attack resolves; initial delay: 2.0s (timer starts at −2.0, so first attack fires at 6s after spawn).
- Added `_nearest_player()`, `_laser_origin()`, `_begin_aoe()`, `_land_aoe()`, `_begin_laser()`, `_fire_laser()` helpers.

**Files changed:**
- `scripts/enemy.gd` — AI system added; state machine, both attack types with indicators, damage resolution.

**Decisions:**
- AOE disc is a child of the enemy node (position expressed in local space) — stays attached if enemy were ever moved.
- Laser beam is added to `get_tree().root` (world space) — avoids needing to account for enemy local transform when computing beam midpoint and orientation. `add_child` is called before setting `global_position`/`look_at` so the global transform resolves correctly.
- `look_at(_laser_to, Vector3.UP)` makes the BoxMesh's local −Z axis point toward the player — BoxMesh length is along Z, so this correctly spans origin→target.
- Laser hit detection: project player position onto beam direction vector, check perpendicular distance ≤ 2.0. Players behind the beam origin are skipped (`proj < 0.0` guard).
- `SHADING_MODE_UNSHADED` on both indicators ensures they render at full brightness regardless of scene lighting — red danger zones stay readable.
- Random 50/50 coin flip selects attack type each cycle.
- Initial delay is 2.0s but first attack fires at 6s total (initial delay + full first cooldown). Intentional — gives the player time to orient.

**Notes:**
- The `players` group must be registered by `player.gd` via `add_to_group("players")` for `take_damage` calls to reach the player. Verify this is in place.
- Beam indicators pulse but don't track the player during windup — target is locked at windup start. This is intentional (telegraphed attack, dodge window matters).
- `_flash_hit()` and the new attack flashes both drive `_body_mat.albedo_color` — they can conflict if hit and attack land simultaneously. At current cooldown/windup values this is very unlikely.

### — Enemy movement + AOE screen shake

**Asked:** Make the Undead Colossus move around near the player in a constant random wander (Monster Hunter style — always in motion, not chasing). AOE stomp should cause screen shake when it lands.

**Done:**
- Changed enemy from `StaticBody3D` → `CharacterBody3D` in both `enemy.gd` and `Enemy.tscn`.
- Added `_move_wander(delta)` called every frame: picks a random waypoint within 3–8 units of the player, moves toward it at 2.8 m/s, picks a new one when within 1.5 units or after 2.5–5.0 seconds.
- Movement uses `move_and_slide()` with flat XZ velocity; gravity applied when not on floor.
- Added `_screen_shake()`: finds the player's Camera3D child, tweens 5 random positional offsets (±0.5 XZ, ±0.25 Y) at 50ms each, then returns camera to rest. Called from `_land_aoe()`.
- Moved AOE disc indicator from child-of-enemy (local space) to world-space root — prevents the disc from drifting with the moving enemy during the 1.4s windup window.

**Files changed:**
- `scenes/Enemy.tscn` — node type changed to `CharacterBody3D`.
- `scripts/enemy.gd` — `extends CharacterBody3D`; movement constants, `_waypoint`/`_waypoint_timer` state; `_move_wander()`, `_pick_waypoint()`, `_screen_shake()` helpers; `_process()` updated to call `_move_wander`; `_land_aoe()` updated to call `_screen_shake()`; AOE indicator moved to scene root.

**Decisions:**
- Waypoints target a random ring around the player (not directly at or away from the player) — feels like constant territorial pacing rather than chasing or fleeing.
- Screen shake duration: 5 × 50ms ticks + 100ms settle = ~350ms total. Short but punchy.
- Camera rest position hardcoded to `Vector3(0, 12, 9)` matching `Player.tscn`. If the camera position is ever changed in the scene file, this must be updated here too.
- AOE disc put in scene root (same approach as laser beam) so it stays fixed in world space while the colossus wanders during windup.

**Notes:**
- The colossus's collision shape (added in `_build_visuals()`) covers it for `move_and_slide()`. No additional collision shape needed in `Enemy.tscn`.
- The thrown `RigidBody3D` projectiles detect hits via `is_in_group("enemies")` + `take_damage()` — unaffected by the body type change.
- `CharacterBody3D` needs `up_direction` set (defaults to `Vector3.UP`) and uses `floor_snap_length` for step detection. Defaults are fine for a flat ground plane.

### — Player respawn + spawn zone markers

**Asked:** When the player dies, respawn at the original spawn position. Spawn positions should show as blue squares on the ground and block building.

**Done:**
- Added `SPAWN_POSITIONS: Array[Vector3]` const and `SPAWN_ZONE_HALF: float = 1.0` to `game_manager.gd`.
- Added AABB guard in `place_object()` — silently blocks placement if the target cell overlaps any spawn zone (±1.0 units on X/Z from spawn center).
- Added `_build_spawn_markers()` to `world.gd` — creates a 2×2 unshaded blue `BoxMesh` (height 0.02) at each spawn position, with alpha transparency and soft blue emission.
- Added `_spawn_pos` and `_dead` vars to `player.gd`. Spawn pos is captured deferred in `_ready()` after the node is positioned.
- `take_damage()` now calls `_die()` when `hp <= 0`. `_die()`: sets `_dead = true`, hides the player node, awaits 1.2 seconds, teleports to `_spawn_pos`, restores `max_hp`, shows the player again, clears `_dead`.
- `_dead` guard added at the top of `_physics_process`, `_unhandled_input`, and `_process`.

**Files changed:**
- `scripts/game_manager.gd` — `SPAWN_POSITIONS`, `SPAWN_ZONE_HALF` consts; no-build AABB check in `place_object()`.
- `scripts/world.gd` — `_build_spawn_markers()` added and called from `_ready()`.
- `scripts/player.gd` — `_spawn_pos`, `_dead` vars; `_store_spawn_pos()` deferred call; `_die()` async respawn; `_dead` guards in process/input.

**Decisions:**
- Spawn zone check is AABB (not radius) to match the square visual exactly. `SPAWN_ZONE_HALF = 1.0` means the 2×2 marker and no-build area are identical.
- `_spawn_pos` stored deferred so it captures the node's final world position after scene placement.
- `await get_tree().create_timer(1.2)` — player is invisible for 1.2s, then snaps back. No tween needed.
- Blue marker uses `SHADING_MODE_UNSHADED` so it reads clearly regardless of lighting.
