# Classes

Classes are not fixed roles — they are starting points. Your stat allocation determines whether you become a Destroyer, a Builder, or something in between.

## Warrior

High Strength — the natural Destroyer. Can solo-lift heavy objects and deal massive throw damage.

| Stat | Base Value |
|---|---|
| Strength | 15 |
| Dexterity | 8 |
| Intelligence | 5 |
| Vitality | 12 |

**Throw Damage Multiplier:** ×1.4

**Lift Capacity Formula:** `strength * 6`

**Starting Skills:** skill-heavy-throw, skill-power-lift, skill-slam

## Engineer

High DEX/INT — the natural Builder. Fast crafting, precise placement, low throw damage.

| Stat | Base Value |
|---|---|
| Strength | 6 |
| Dexterity | 14 |
| Intelligence | 13 |
| Vitality | 7 |

**Throw Damage Multiplier:** ×0.8

**Lift Capacity Formula:** `strength * 4`

**Starting Skills:** skill-fast-craft, skill-blueprint, skill-reinforce
