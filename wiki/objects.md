# Objects

Placeable objects can be picked up, stored in your cart, thrown at enemies, or built into fortifications.
Raw materials can also be thrown but deal reduced damage.

## Placeable Objects

| Object | Weight | Solo Liftable | Throw Tier |

### Throw Tiers

| Tier | Impact |
|---|---|
| Light | Low damage, great for quick harassment |
| Medium | Reliable damage, suits most encounters |
| Heavy | Serious damage, worth the extra cart weight |
| Massive | Catastrophic — reserved for coordinated party throws |

### Object Descriptions

**Flower Pot** *(5 kg)*

A ceramic flower pot. Light and easy to throw solo. More demoralizing than deadly.

**Iron Fence Section** *(15 kg)*

A decorative iron fence section. Sharp edges make it effective when thrown.

**Wooden Crate** *(20 kg)*

A solid wooden crate. Light enough for most to throw solo.

**Garden Bench** *(25 kg)*

A sturdy wooden bench. An excellent mid-range throw weapon.

**Cherry Tree** *(40 kg)*

A decorative cherry tree. A well-placed throw scatters petals and damage into enemies.

**Stone Wall Section** *(60 kg)*

A section of dressed stone wall. Snaps to the build grid perfectly. Can fortify or flatten.

**Stone Block** *(80 kg)*

A precisely shaped quarry block. At the edge of solo lift capacity — devastating when thrown.

**Iron Spike Trap** *(35 kg)*

A folded iron trap with sharpened spikes. Surprisingly light for how much damage it deals. Even more dangerous placed on the ground.

**Small Shed** *(120 kg)*

A small storage shed with a corrugated roof. Heavy, but a strong Warrior can still manage solo.

**Wooden House** *(200 kg)*

A full timber-frame house. No solo player has ever lifted one. That is the point.

## Raw Materials

Materials deal significantly reduced damage when thrown. They are intended as crafting inputs, not weapons.

| Material | Weight | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Stone Chunk** | 10 kg | Raw stone. Heavy enough to hurt someone but not shaped for combat. |
| **Wood Plank** | 4 kg | A flat plank of wood. Good for building. Poor as a weapon. |
| **Iron Ingot** | 8 kg | Smelted iron. Dense and throwable, but shaped for building not fighting. |
