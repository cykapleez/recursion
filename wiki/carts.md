# Carts

Your cart stores the objects you pick up. Upgrading your cart increases capacity and unlocks perk slots.

| Cart | Tier | Max Weight | Perk Slots | Overload Penalty |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Standard Cart** | 1 | 150 kg | 2 | −35% speed |
| **Reinforced Cart** | 2 | 250 kg | 3 | −30% speed |
| **Siege Cart** | 3 | 400 kg | 4 | −25% speed |

## Standard Cart

A basic wooden cart. Gets the job done but fills up fast.

*No craft cost — available from the start.*

## Reinforced Cart

Iron-braced frame with wider wheels. Holds more and handles overload better.

**Craft Cost:**
- 8× item-iron-ingot
- 4× item-wood-plank

## Siege Cart

A massive hauling platform built for carrying the heaviest crafted weapons. Slow when overloaded — but the perks make it a monster.

**Craft Cost:**
- 20× item-iron-ingot
- 10× item-stone-slab
- 8× item-wood-plank
