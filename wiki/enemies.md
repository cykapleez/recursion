# Enemies

Enemies threaten your structures and must be defeated using throw and smash mechanics.

> **Note:** Monster combat values are intentionally not listed here. Discover their strength through play.

## Test Monster

A stocky crimson-skinned creature with curved dark horns and glowing yellow eyes. Built like a wall, moves like one too.

**Threat Level:** Moderate

**Behavior:** Stationary. Waits for players to bring the fight to it. Does not pursue or retaliate in the current build.

**Weaknesses:** thrown objects, smash attacks

**Tips:**
- A Smash attack deals significantly more damage than a throw — get close if you can.
- The Iron Spike Trap is the most damage-efficient throw for its weight.
- Heavier objects hit harder. A Wooden House from a coordinated party is a finishing blow.
